# Image Folder

Add your photo here as `photo.jpg` or `photo.png`.

The image will be displayed as a 224px circular photo on the Hero section.

**Recommended:**
- File name: `photo.jpg` or `photo.png`
- Size: 500x500px or larger (square format works best for circles)
- Format: JPG or PNG
- File size: Under 500KB for best performance

Once you add your image file, refresh your browser and the circular photo will appear on the left side of your portfolio.
